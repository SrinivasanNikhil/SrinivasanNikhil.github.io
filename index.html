Dr. Nikhil Srinivasan


I am a lecturer at the Management Information Systems (MIS) department at the Terry College of Business at the University of Georgia. I teach a courses at the undergraduate and the graduate level ranging from computer programming in Java to data analytics and visualization and cyber security. I also teach online Masters courses with a focus on Database Management and web based client-side development using Javascript and React. I also serve as a supervisor and faculty mentor to graduate projects in the Masters of Business Analytics and Masters of Business and Technology program.
