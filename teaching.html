Teaching

As a Senior Lecturer in the MIS Department at the Terry College of Business, University of Georgia, I have been committed to providing high-quality instruction in Information Systems and Business Analytics. My teaching philosophy focuses on fostering hands-on, practical skills in students while integrating industry trends and real-world applications.

Courses Taught

University of Georgia (2013 - Present)

  • Data Management (Undergraduate & Graduate) – SQL, database design, normalization, and cloud-based data solutions.
  • Computer Programming in Business – Introduction to programming concepts, Python, and JavaScript for business applications.
  • Project Management – Agile methodologies, IT project planning, and risk management.
  • Database Management (MIST7510E) – Advanced SQL, data warehousing, and database security.
  • Technology Capstone Project (MIST7590E) – Applied technology solutions for business challenges.
  • Internet Programming II (MIST7571E) – Web development with modern frameworks and APIs.
  • Introduction to Information Systems (MIST2090) – Overview of IT infrastructure, enterprise systems, and data security.
  • Data Analytics (MIST7600) – Business analytics using Python, R, and Tableau.
  • IT Infrastructure (MIST5640) – Cloud computing, network security, and IT operations.
  • SQL for Business Analytics (BUSN3020E) – Practical SQL applications in business environments.
