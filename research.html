PhD in Information Systems

Research

My research explores how technology shapes organizations and society—from social network dynamics to big data analytics and information systems development.

Research Interests

Social Network Analysis

Studying innovation and information diffusion through network structures. How ideas spread and influence emerges in digital platforms.

Big Data Analytics

Techniques for extraction, analysis, and visualization of large-scale data. Making sense of complexity through computational methods.

IS Design & Development

IT architecture, software engineering practices, and user experience. Building systems that work for people and organizations.
